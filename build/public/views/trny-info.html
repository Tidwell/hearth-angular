This is a {{tournament.tournament.signupCap}}-player {{tournament.tournament.tournamentType}} tournament. If you lose a round, your are eliminated. This is a region tournament.

Each round is played best-of-three games (the first player to win 2 games wins the round).

Depending on how far you advance, this tournament could take anywhere between 45 minutes and 2 hours. Please ensure you have enough time to complete the tournament.

  • You should have decks for at least three classes
  • Players choose a deck at the beginning of the round
  • The winning player continues with the same deck
  • The losing player must switch class
If you close the HSTRNY site and do not reconnect within 4 minutes you will be dropped from the tournament.
